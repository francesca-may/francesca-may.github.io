Clothing References

Maxi's magical girl outfit is inspired by a mashup of various clothing items!

  • Here is the reference that I used for her collar. She also has a medium sized bow in the middle of the collar with a cute dinosaur on it (dino reference further down)!
  • The details on the dress and sleeves are reminiscent of this dress.

  • Her petticoat shows underneath her skirt too it is white and fluffy.

  • Other than that, she has a dinosaur clip in her hair. I imagine the clip to be a mashup of these - this for the spikes and this for the face!

Additional Appearance Information

  • Maxi’s skin tone is quite pale, and she has freckles on her nose.
  • She has heterochromia - her left eye is pale blue and her other is a light grey.
  • Her hair is inspired by these two characters; Tatsumaki from One Punch Man and Kofuku from Noragami. I like to style her hair in the direction of Tatsumaki but the stylistic curls of Kofuku.
  • Her hair is white with the front curl being blue. Her colour design is based on my computer case which is a CiT Dragon³ White with Blue Fans, so the colour scheme of this particular illustration by Inioli is what the best match.

Personality

Maxi is quite short, and because of this she can sometimes try to balance that with an outgoing personality. She is witty and sarcastic (some would say cheeky) but genuinely kind. She generally knows when she is too much, and although it takes a lot for her to lose it, she sometimes has a bit of a temper. Overall, she is a free spirit.

Suro

Clothing References

Suro is wearing pyjamas! The colour of these pyjamas are green, they are patterned with simple cecropia leaves; they are more in the general shape as opposed to a detailed rendition.

  • Her pyjama top has a collar which is in this style. The sleeves are super long, past her hands.
  • Her pyjama bottoms are in this style.
  • She wears long white socks underneath her pyjama bottoms.

Additional Appearance Information

This character is based on a sloth. This is why she is sleepy/dozy looking!

  • Suro's hair is in a bob which goes to her shoulders.
  • Her face has two marks from the side of her face to her eyes (mimicking sloth faces).
  • She is around the ages of 19-23 (not concrete yet).

Personality

Suro loves to be cosy and doesn’t like a lot of noise or excitement, as she finds it overwhelming. Very soft. She is quiet and reserved, if she isn't sleeping, she's reading a book or having a tea party with her plushies.
