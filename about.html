About Me

Hi there! My name is Francesca, but I commonly go as ‘Fran’!

I am from the UK, but during my highschool life I have lived in Doha, Qatar and Dubai, UAE.
I moved back to the UK to persue a degree in Computing BSc (Hons) in 2014 where I found my true passion for Web Design and Development, as well as Graphic Design. I have always been creative, so when I made my first ‘proper’ website in the first year of my course, I was extremely excited to get stuck in and make cool things!

My Skills

I am most confident with font-end skills such as HTML5, CSS3, SASS & jQuery.

I have experience with back-end skills such as PHP, PDO & MySQL, though I may need a little encouragement or training depending on the project. I have experience with Adobe CC; namely photoshop, premiere pro and dreamweaver. I have little experience with ilustrator, though I do have experience with inkscape which is based on the same functionalities.
